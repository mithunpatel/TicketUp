Our Story

Everyday, there’s a reason to go to a pub, wouldn’t you agree? Whether you’re happy or sad, you’re always heading towards your local watering hole - well, at least we did. It happens to the bestl of us! We thought, if it’s such a recurring ordeal, it might as well be planned We thought - “there has to be a smarter way to drink up, have a great experience, all while saving a buck or two” -. And then it struck is - if you buy booze beforehand, you can then always make a plan (because every good plan starts with a drink). You save a buck and voila! Before you know it, you’re in high spirits with your friends!
